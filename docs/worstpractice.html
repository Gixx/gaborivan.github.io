WorstPractice Project

For a long time now I always felt I want to shout out to the world all the crazy ideas I have in my mind. But I know that I am too shy to present them with big face and loud voice. I would never be able stand in front of other people to a speak abut technical topics. I am not a telling man. I won't be followed by millions, not even tens. I don’t feel empowered to talk about best practices. But I still want to share my experiences.

This is how the Worst Practice project was born. The name is protecting me. The humor, the sarcasm and self-criticism in the articles are protecting me. The worst thing can happen is, I write something really bullshit, that is - true to its name - a worst practice.
